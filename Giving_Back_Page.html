Giving Back

Everyone deserves a safe and happy home

Over 20 million Americans are in need of structurally necessary home repairs and remodels.

1.65 million of our neighbors struggle with the health and economic implications of living in unsafe and unstable living conditions.

Maykke is partnering with Rebuilding Together, a leading nonprofit committed rebuilding homes and revitalizing the communities of our neighbors in need. Their 130+ local affiliates provide critical repairs to the homes of our neighbors and revitalize the communities in which we live. Since 1988 Rebuilding Together has served 5.78 million people, repairing and rehabilitating 192,000+ homes and nonprofit facilities in 39 states and the District of Columbia.

We want to make a difference, so we are donating time, money, and Maykke products to Rebuilding Together projects in our native San Francisco Bay Area and around the country.
